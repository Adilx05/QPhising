# Trademark Policy

The names, logos, product identity, and branding associated with QPhising may be used in community forks, references, documentation, comparisons, integrations, and compatible distributions.

## Allowed

You may:

- Refer to the original project as QPhising
- State that your fork is based on QPhising
- Use the name in documentation such as:
  - "Powered by QPhising"
  - "Forked from QPhising"
  - "Compatible with QPhising"

## Not Allowed

You may not falsely imply:

- Official endorsement
- Partnership
- Ownership of the original project
- That your modified version is the official upstream release

## Clarification

Using the QPhising name is allowed. Removal, censorship, renaming, or forced rebranding is not required under this policy.

Only misleading or deceptive branding is prohibited.
