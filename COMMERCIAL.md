# Commercial Licensing

QPhising is available under the Community License for non-commercial use.

If your intended use involves commercial activity, revenue generation, client delivery, or paid services, a separate commercial license is required.

## Examples Requiring Commercial License

- Selling QPhising installations
- Charging for hosted access (SaaS)
- Reselling modified versions
- Paid consulting where QPhising is the delivered product
- Bundling QPhising into a commercial security platform
- White-label resale
- Managed services built primarily around QPhising

## Examples That Do Not Require Commercial License

- Internal evaluation
- Educational labs
- Research usage
- Personal experimentation
- Internal non-revenue operational use

## Requesting a Commercial License

Contact the project owner with:

- Company / Organization name
- Intended use case
- Deployment scale
- Commercial model
- Support requirements

Commercial terms may include:

- Redistribution rights
- OEM / white-label rights
- SLA / support
- Priority fixes
- Private feature development
